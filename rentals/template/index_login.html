About us

Bike rentals is a shared mobility offering that allows users to own the vehicle for only the duration of their need and return after it.
It is a much better option economically. Users do not have to invest a huge sum of money to buy vehicles and instead spend a small amount as and when they need a two wheeler. Users also need not
take the burden of insurance and maintenance. We also provide 24/7 roadside assistance to customers who rent a bike in Bangalore. Two wheelers on rent in Bangalore is a much safer
alternative to public transport. Users can travel to places without being dependent on anyone else while also maintaining social distance. Bike rentals in Bangalore is
also a safer alternative to cabs because the user doesn’t share the ride with anyone else.
